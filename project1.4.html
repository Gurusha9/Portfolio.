4. The Vanishing Hour - Mystery Story Poster

Description:
This poster was created for The Vanishing Hour, an original mystery story concept. The overarching theme revolves around time, memory, and disappearance. A large clock face dominates the composition, while the central silhouette hints at the missing protagonist.

Design Focus:

  • Golden monochrome palette to reflect nostalgia and fading memory.
  • Minimal silhouette to maintain suspense and ambiguity.
  • Symmetry and balance to emphasize time as the core motif.
  • typographic hierarchy for a cinematic feel.

Outcome:
A dramatic, atmospheric poster that communicates genre, tone, and narrative tension effectively, showing my ability to design for storytelling formats.
